Refer Your Friends & Associates

Do you know someone who would really benefit from attending an Intensive Journal workshop?

The Journal can help practically anyone who has an interest in developing his/her life. Some examples of individuals who are likely to be interested in the method are:

  • Going through a transition
  • Interested in mind/ body/ spirit connections
  • Looking for ways to increase creativity
  • Interested in working with dreams and imagery
  • Counselors, psychologists, nurses, social workers

Many Intensive Journal workshops have been successful for larger groups including: women's/ men's groups, writers, churches, and senior citizens. Consider these as well for referrals.

Personal referrals are very helpful to us in continuing to offer the program.

Please fill out the referral form below and we'll send them introductory literature.

Thanks for your help.
