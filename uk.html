Location Ashburnham Place is a leading retreat and conference centre that is located south of London, 5 miles west of the town of Battle within the county of East Sussex. see www.ashburnham.org.uk. The centre has spacious grounds (220 acres) with walking areas and ponds that provide a beautiful setting. Our conference and lodging will be in the Patmos Lodge, a very quiet setting that is appropriate for our program.

The centre is accessible by train from London; there is hourly service from Charing Cross and Waterloo East to Battle via Sevenoaks and Tonbridge. The train station in Battle is 5 miles to the centre by taxi. There is also hourly train service from Victoria to Bexhill via Chapham Junction, East Croydon, Gatwick and Eastbourne. The Bexhill station is 7 miles from Ashburnham Place by taxi.
